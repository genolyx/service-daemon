Methods and Limitations

Cell-free DNA (cfDNA) extracted from maternal plasma is captured using a hybridization-based target enrichment protocol and sequenced on an Illumina platform. Reads are aligned to GRCh38. Variant calling is performed using validated bioinformatics pipelines. Fetal-specific variants are identified using fetal fraction estimation and allele-fraction analysis. The minimum fetal fraction required for reliable analysis is 4%. Variant classification follows ACMG/AMP 2015 guidelines. This test interrogates coding regions and splice sites (±20 bp) of the targeted gene(s). This assay does not detect copy-number variants, trinucleotide repeat expansions, or variants outside the targeted regions. A negative result does not exclude all genetic conditions.
